Designers 4 Divas provides fashion shows for local events and charities.

What to Expect:

During our fashion shows, our models show off our latest products. Our products are handcrafted by local designer, Janie Harris. Janie, former pastor, founded Designers 4 Divas with the core value being modesty with style. Our fashion shows reflect her values, leading to family friendly and uplifting events that our team has put on many times over the past few years.
