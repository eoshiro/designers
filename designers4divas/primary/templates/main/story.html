About Us:

Designers 4 Divas was founded by designer, Janie Harris. Janie learned how to create early. While growing up in a farm, as the third oldest out of eleven children, she was able to learn how to sew from her mother. She credits her mother, who made clothes for her and her siblings, as an early inspiration for her creations.

Janie has made clothes for people throughout her entire life. She has a giving spirit which led to her spending years working in foster/daycare and later moved on to become a pastor. She believes strongly in modesty, and wishes to create a more stylish modesty in today's culture.
